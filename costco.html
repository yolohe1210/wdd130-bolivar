Costco

Costco is a unique shopping experience. It is incredibly easy to lose sight of your goals there. Here are some tips to keep your Costco runs under control:

▪︎ Do Not Shop Hungry

This advice is useful for any shopping outing, it is especially important at Costco. Shopping hungry can lead to impulse buys and overshopping. Luckily, Costco offers a built in solution in the food court. If you ever end up at Costco hungry, spending a few dollars at the food court before shopping can save you dozens in preventable mistakes.

▪︎ Know How Much You can Handle

Many of the deals in Costco cannot be matched anywhere, but this does not mean they are all the right fit for you. If your household cannot take care of a bulk item before it goes bad, then it is out of your budget. If you are throwing away products, you are throwing away money.
